# Email Style Notes

Use these rules for article-analysis emails sent to the research list.

## Delivery mode

- Send one email per recipient.
- Do not send a combined multi-recipient email.
- Personalize the opening so the recipient feels the note was curated for them.

## Email structure

Use this order exactly:
1. TL;DR
2. Key Takeaways
3. Detailed Breakdown

Formatting rules:
- Do not number the section labels.
- Do not write `Standard Format`.
- Keep the HTML clean, readable, and polished.
- Attach the original source image.

## Opening style

Start with a short note such as:
- Specially curated for you based on today’s article.
- Sharing this concise note for your quick review.
- I pulled together this short brief for you from today’s article.

Vary the wording naturally so every email does not feel templated.
