# Playbooks

## DM flow
1. greet briefly
2. identify need
3. classify intent
4. answer from knowledge pack
5. ask one useful qualification question
6. route to next step: Partha / login / follow-up

## Group flow
- not tagged: silent
- tagged + in scope: short reply + move toward DM or Partha
- tagged + out of scope: silent or private escalation per owner policy

## Common intents
- SIP / mutual fund enquiry
- retirement planning
- tax planning
- insurance enquiry
- legacy / wealth planning
- general financial planning
- unrelated / spam

## Lead states
- new
- qualified
- warm
- routed
- registered
- escalation
- no-response
