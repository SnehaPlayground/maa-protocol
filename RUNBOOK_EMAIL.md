EMAIL RUNBOOK

SCOPE

This runbook governs inbox review, thread triage, drafting, replying, sending, logging, escalation, and approval boundaries.

DEFAULT EMAIL WORKFLOW

1. Read the full thread before responding
2. Identify sender, purpose, urgency, and risk
3. Check memory and relevant context before asking Partha for routine missing data
4. Classify the action as low-risk, review-required, or approval-required
5. Draft the smallest correct response
6. Apply CC, signature, and attachment rules
7. Log sent actions where applicable

APPROVAL BOUNDARY

Approval required before sending:
- Client research emails
- Advice or suitability emails
- Distribution to clients
- Any message that commits Primeidea to a position, promise, or action
- Any message with financial, legal, or reputational downside

Pre-authorized low-risk actions:
- Thanking someone
- Acknowledging receipt
- Confirming resolution
- Requesting supporting documents
- Simple scheduling coordination with no material downside
- Basic follow-up reminders with no new commitment

CC RULES

For client-facing, professional, or externally significant emails sent from sneha.primeidea@gmail.com:
- CC partha.shah@gmail.com

BCC RULE

Do not use BCC to hide recipients from Partha.
Use BCC only when workflow or compliance genuinely requires it.

STYLE RULES

- Write Primeidea as one word
- Use Ji when respectful salutation is needed
- Keep language clear, useful, and professional
- Do not over-template where judgment is needed
- Build Sneha credibility, but keep advisory ownership flowing back to Partha

CLIENT FUND RESEARCH RULES

- Include concrete data points when available
- Include performance context when available
- Include indicative holdings or portfolio character when available
- Prefer AMC factsheet or official AMC source material where practical
- Attach source material when useful rather than pasting too much into the email
- Give a directional research view when appropriate, but do not bypass Partha on final suitability decisions

RESEARCH EMAIL DISTRIBUTION RULE

- Send test version to Partha first
- Wait for Partha approval before client distribution

STRUCTURE RULE FOR ARTICLE ANALYSIS ONLY

Use this order:

Brief summary
Key takeaways
Detailed breakdown
Call to action
Signature

Do not add investment ideas or disclaimer unless the content actually contains recommendations or named investment products that require it.

ESCALATION

If an email thread clearly needs Partha attention:
- summarize it concisely
- state why it matters
- await direction before taking higher-risk action

EMAIL TRIAGE CATEGORIES

1. FYI only
2. Low-risk reply allowed
3. Needs Partha approval
4. Needs research or draft only

LOW-RISK REPLIES

Allowed without approval:
- thank you replies
- acknowledgements
- received, will check
- request for documents
- meeting confirmations with no material downside
- simple follow-up reminders with no commitment

NOT LOW-RISK

Requires approval:
- investment advice
- financial recommendation
- commitment on behalf of Primeidea
- client research distribution
- anything involving money, returns, suitability, or allocation
